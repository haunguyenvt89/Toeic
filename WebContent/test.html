Directions: You will hear a question or statement and three responses spoken in English. They will not be printed in your test book and will be spoken only one time. Select the best response to the question or statement and select (A), (B), or (C) to mark your answer on the answer sheet.

Example:
You will hear: Where did you last see Ms. Montgomery?
You will also hear:
(A) Yes, I can see it on the flat screen.
(B) It was about three weeks ago.
(C) At the First Street coffee shop.
The best response to the question "Where did you last see Ms. Montgomery? " is choice (C), "At the First Street coffee shop." so (C) is the correct answer. You should mark answer (C).
